The substitution is the relatively obvious replacement \(v = \frac{y}{t}\text{.}\) The right side of the equation easily turns into \(f(v)\text{,}\) but the transformation of the left side is trickier.

\begin{align*} y \amp = tv\\ f(v) = \frac{dy}{dt} \amp = v + t \frac{dv}{dt}\\ \frac{dv}{dt} \amp = \frac{f(v) - v}{t}\\ \int \frac{dv}{f(v)-v} \amp = \int \frac{dt}{t} \end{align*}
