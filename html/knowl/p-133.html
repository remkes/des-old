Before moving on with other techniques for solving first order equations, this is a nice place to take a pure-mathematical detour and talk about existence and uniqueness of solutions. We're going to deal with first order equations where we can isolate the derivative term, that is, equations of the form

\begin{equation*} \frac{dy}{dx} = F(x,y)\text{.} \end{equation*}
