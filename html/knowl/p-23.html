We use the ratio or root tests to calculate the radius of convergence of a series. After some manipulation of those tests, (if the coefficients are non-zer) the radius of convergence is given by the formula

\begin{equation*} R = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \left| \frac{c_n}{c_{n+1}} \right| = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt[n]{|c_n|}}\text{.} \end{equation*}
