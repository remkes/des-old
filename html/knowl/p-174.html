The roots of \((p-7)^2 \sinh (p-3)\) are \(p=3\) and \(p=7\text{.}\) The right side evaluated at \(p=1\) is negative, so the trajectory on \((0,3)\) is downward. The right side evaluated at \(p=5\) is positive, so the trajectory on \((3,7)\) is upward. The right side evaluated at \(p=7\) is positive, so the trajectory on \((7, \infty)\) is upward.
