\begin{equation*} \frac{dp}{dt} = p^2 - p \end{equation*}

The right side is zero when \(p=0\) or \(p=1\text{,}\) so those are the steady states. When \(p \in (0,1)\) the derivative is negative, so the trajectory is decreasing.
