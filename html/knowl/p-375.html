The solution constructed above is written \(J_\nu\) and called the Bessel function of the first kind of order \(\nu\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} J_{\nu} = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{n! \Gamma(1+n+\nu)} \left( \frac{t}{2} \right)^{2n+\nu} \end{equation*}

It converges on \((0, \infty)\text{.}\) It may not be defined at 0 or negative numbers. In general, the applications are only interested in the Bessel function on the domain \((0, \infty)\text{.}\) The Bessel functions of the first kind for \(\nu \in \ZZ\) are show in FigureÂ 4.6.3.
