The convolution behaves well with differentiation.

\begin{equation*} \frac{d}{dt} (f \star g) = \frac{df}{dt} \star g + f \star \frac{dg}{dt} \end{equation*}

It also behaves well with integration.

\begin{equation*} \int_0^\infty f \star g dt = \int_0^\infty f(t) dt \int_0^\infty g(t) dt \end{equation*}
