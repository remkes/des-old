Examples exist in other disciplines as well. If we go back to physics, we can look at the physics of the coupled spring. In this sytem, we have a mass \(m_1\) on a spring with spring constant \(k_1\) attached to a fixed object. Then we have a second mass \(m_2\) attached to the first by a spring with spring constant \(k_2\text{.}\) Analyzing the forces and using Newton's law of motion results in a system of DEs.

\begin{align*} m_1x_1^{\prime \prime} \amp = - k_1x_1 + k_2(x_2 - x_1)\\ m_2x_2^{\prime \prime} \amp = -k_2(x_2-x_1) \end{align*}
