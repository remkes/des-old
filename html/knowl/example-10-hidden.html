This example is a specific instance of a form known as the logistic equation.

\begin{equation*} \frac{dp}{dt} = 4p-p^2 \end{equation*}

The right side is zero when \(p=0\) or \(p=4\text{,}\) so those are the steady states. When \(p \in (0,4)\) the derivative is positive, so the trajectory is increasing.
