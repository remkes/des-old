Again, this is very convenient. The derivatives are completely removed in the \(s\)-domain, replaced with simple algebraic operations. The general result for any order of differentiation is

\begin{equation*} \calL \left\{ \frac{d^n f}{dt^n} \right\} = s^n F(s) - s^{n-1} f(0) - s^{n-2} f^\prime(0) - s^{n-3} f^{\prime \prime}(0) - \ldots - f^{(n-1)}(0)\text{.} \end{equation*}
